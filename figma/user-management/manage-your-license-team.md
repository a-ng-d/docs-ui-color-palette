# Manage your license・/team

UI Color Palette /team supports activation through **license keys**, designed for team-wide deployments and seat management.

## Activate with a license key

1. Open `Manage license` in the `User menu` <i class="fa-user">:user:</i>.
2. Paste your license key in the **Instance key** field and enter a name to identify the seat.
3. Click **Activate**.

Each seat must be assigned to a unique user. License keys can be assigned or revoked at any time by the license owner.

{% hint style="warning" %}
New license key sales are suspended. Existing keys remain fully supported.
{% endhint %}
